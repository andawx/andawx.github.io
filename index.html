What to Pack

Essentials:

  • Passport and visas (if required)
  • Flight and accommodation confirmations
  • Travel insurance details
  • Medications and prescriptions
  • Phone and charger
  • Universal adapter
  • Comfortable walking shoes

Clothing (adapt to your destination):

  • T-shirts and tops
  • Pants or skirts
  • Sweater or jacket
  • Underwear and socks
  • Swimwear (if applicable)

Toiletries:

  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Shampoo, conditioner, and soap
  • Sunscreen and insect repellent
  • Personal hygiene items

Navigating Airport Checks

Before You Go:

  • Check your flight status online.
  • Ensure your baggage meets the airline's size and weight restrictions.
  • Have your boarding pass and ID ready.

At the Airport:

  • Check-in at the airline counter or online.
  • Proceed to security screening:
    • Remove electronics and liquids from your bag.
    • Take off your belt and shoes (as required).
  • Find your gate and be there on time for boarding.

Getting Around

Common Options:

  • Public Transportation (buses, trains, subways)
  • Taxis and ride-sharing services
  • Rental cars
  • Walking and cycling

Tips:

  • Research transportation options at your destination in advance.
  • Consider purchasing travel passes for public transport.
  • Download relevant transportation apps.
